Panwadi

A village of 5 tribes coming together having a population of more than 500, is a sweet and small village based near the feet of Purandar fort. Tribus reached this village with 4 volunteers and bundles of donation. Books, slates, clothes and much more. We had a beautiful session with the Zilla Parishad school over there having, group discussions and speeches, talking about the beauty of nature and the fort above them and the legacy of Shivaji Maharaj.
